Summer Questions

Report Cards

Report Cards will be published to Aspen family portals before the end of June. Mr. Pereyra will send an email when they are available to be viewed.

When will my Transcript be sent to my College?

Once report cards are finalized Ms. Deknatel will send transcripts directly to your school. Feel free to email her with questions. Remember that you are responsible for requesting your Middlesex Community College transcript.

Summer Classes

Students that failed Semester 2 classes will be emailed information on credit recovery after report cards have been published.

How do I get a Work Permit?

Ms. Kelly and Ms. Crystal will be at school over the summer albeit on reduced hours. Complete this MA State Work Permit Application and email it to both Ms. Kelly and Ms. Crystal. (Your employer completes page 1; You/your parents complete page 2) One of us will let you know when the permit is ready to pick up. PLEASE allow 3 days for processing.

I’m going for my driving permit and need proof from the school!

You will need a copy of your school transcript and a 2nd form of ID. The school can provide you with a letter if you don’t have a student ID/passport. Email Ms. Crystal and Ms. Kelly and one of us will get the paperwork ready for you. Please allow 3 days for processing.

When is the first day of school?

The first day of school will be Tuesday, August 27th. Check your email over the summer for information about the Ice Cream Social and volunteer opportunities at Orientation.
